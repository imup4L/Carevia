FAQ
How fast can a shift be confirmed?
Many shifts can be confirmed the same day. Timing depends on clinic needs and role availability — but the process is designed to be fast and clear.
Do you only serve Seattle?
CareVia is Seattle-area focused — including nearby neighborhoods and cities like Bellevue and Kirkland — so the matching experience stays local and practical.
What roles are supported?
Common roles include Dental Hygienists, Dental Assistants, and Front Desk / Administrative support.
Is CareVia free for dental professionals?
Yes — creating a profile and browsing shifts is completely free for dental professionals. Clinics are billed separately for filled placements.
